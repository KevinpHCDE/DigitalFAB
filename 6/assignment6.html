Lamps Part 2 and Molds!

This was a productive assignment!

I 3d printed molds to cast silicone in to make molds to cast plaster in. It took me sustained effort to understand the meta-ness of the assignment. What needs to be a positive or a negative was hard done than said. It took more effort to actually produce. After the struggle lifted, I was able to enjoy this assignment and mess about with, paint, epoxy and casting wax into the molds.

Design question remained from last week: How can I make this struggle playful and trust the design process to enable something worthwile to immerge? How might I make something small enough that I can make mistakes and not run out of materials, (like the silicone and plaster), and have time to be to free to experiment and play?
