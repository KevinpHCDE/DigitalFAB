Kevin Philbin's HCDE 598 Digital Fabrication Page!

My name is Kevin Philbin! I'm an HCDE Master's student.

I'm most excited about discursive and speculative design for imagining possible and alternative worlds. In this class, I'd like to stretch my conceptual wings and learn how to use 3d printing to make artifacts for better storytelling and product design.

I come from a video background creating commercial and documentary work. If I can, I would like to assist others with using video as a tool of design that can show their work and tell their story.

As you can see, I like swords and photobombing bands. This band I met randomly at the temple on top of Mount Qincheng, Chendu, Sichuan Province, China. This is the birthplace of Taoism. I was in Sichuan for a video project covering China's National Parks and Panda protection. Aawwww!

I'm married to Laura, and have two kids, Seamus, 6 and Barrett 4. They probably want me to 3D print them Legos or light sabres. So that's cool since I like swords. And Legos.
